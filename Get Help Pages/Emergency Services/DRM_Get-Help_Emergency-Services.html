Meals

If you or your loved ones are in immediate need of food, you are welcome to dine at our Lawrence Street Community Center, open every day of the year. The center serves three meals daily:

Breakfast: 6:30 A.M.
Lunch: 11:30 P.M.
Dinner: 6:30 P.M.

Overnight Shelter

For Men

If you are homelessness and want to escape the dangers of sleeping on the street, you are welcome at our Lawrence Street Shelter. The shelter is open 365 days a year and can accommodate up to 315 men each night.

An additional 335 beds are available to men in need at an overflow shelter owned by the City of Denver and operated by the Mission. Guests must sign up at the Lawrence Street Shelter to be entered into the bed assignment system. Beds are assigned randomly across both facilities.

For more information, contact us at 303.297.1815.

For Women and Children

If you are a woman experiencing homelessness and are seeking immediate shelter for you and your children, please call 303.297.1815 to be referred to an appropriate shelter.

Restrooms & Showers

Restrooms

Clean restrooms are available to anyone in need at our Lawrence Street Community Center from 6:30 A.M. to 9:00 P.M. daily.

Showers

Showers are also available to both men and women in need at our Lawrence Street Community Center.

Shower Sign-ups

Showers are available on a first-come first-serve basis, and guests must sign up for a shower in advance. Shower sign-ups are available from 8:30 A.M. to 2:00 P.M..

Shower Availability

Shower time slots are available for guests from 8:00 A.M. to 4:30 P.M.

Resource Room

If you need a towel, soap, or other hygiene items, the Resource Room is available to provide these items free of charge from 8:00 A.M. to 5:00 P.M..

Laundry

Our laundry services are available through the Resource Room at the Lawrence Street Community Center.

The laundry services are drop-off only. You will receive a ticket when they drop off your laundry. Once your laundry is washed and dried, you can turn in your laundry ticket to pick up your clothing.

Laundry services are available from 8:00 A.M. to 5:00 P.M..

Basic Health Services

Please be advised that Mission program participants receive priority scheduling and services. Always call first or visit the clinic to schedule and confirm appointments. To learn more, please call the clinic at 303.294.0157 or visit the clinic, which is located inside our Lawrence Street Shelter.

Clinic Hours

Monday through Friday: 8:30 A.M. to 4:00 P.M.

Saturday: 8:30 A.M. to 12:00 P.M.

Services Available

The following services are available to those in need who cannot receive them under any other coverage. For our guests from the general public, we only offer the following acute services.

Medical

Complimentary over-the-counter medicine samples are available on a limited basis.

Optical

As a guest, you may receive a complimentary optical eye exam (limited availability). Guests who are not participating in our programs will need a current prescription and will pay a nominal fee for both single lens and bifocal eyeglasses (1-pair per 12-month period).

Dental

Complimentary dental extractions are open to guests who are not participating in our programs with scheduled appointments and based on availability. General dental repair is reserved for Mission program participants only.

Chiropractic

Complimentary chiropractic services are offered with scheduled appointments

Miscellaneous

Complimentary HIV testing performed monthly and seasonal flu shots are usually available.
