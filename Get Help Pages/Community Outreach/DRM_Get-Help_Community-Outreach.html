Food Pantry/Hygiene Items

If you and your family cannot afford food at this time, the Mission can provide you with food and hygiene items from our food pantry once a month. Harvest Farm also offers food boxes for people in need near Wellington, Colorado.

Please be ready to present a valid photo ID before you receive food or hygiene items.

Ministry Outreach Center (Denver)

The food pantry is available at the Ministry Outreach center from 8:30 AM to 11:30 AM Monday through Friday (closed holidays).

Clothing

If you or your loved ones need clothing, we invite you to visit our Ministry Outreach Center in Denver or Mom’s Closet at Harvest Farm in Wellington to receive free clothing.

Please be ready to present a valid photo ID before you receive clothing. Clothing sizes and availability is subject to change based on donations from the community.

Furniture

If you and your loved ones are in need of furniture and other household items such as blankets, pillows, towels, and sheets, please contact our Ministry Outreach Center for assistance.

Furniture may be received, free of charge, by appointment only. You must also have a referral from a Social Services agency, church leader or other community representative. At your appointment, you will be given 30 minutes to choose the furniture and items you need.

Please be aware that each family may receive furniture only once every 5 years, and the Mission will not provide transportation or delivery.

Furniture is distributed at the Ministry Outreach Center, by appointment only,
from 12:30 P.M. to 2:00 P.M. Monday through Friday.

Community Outreach Events

Community outreach events are special events hosted by the Mission every year to promote the work we do at the Mission and help people in need during a difficult time in their lives. Through these events, families are able to celebrate holidays together like Thanksgiving and Christmas, and our annual fall festival provides family fun every October while informing people about the important work happening in the lives of men in our New Life Program at Harvest Farm.

 

Harvest Farm Fall Festival

The Harvest Farm Fall Festival is an annual event held at Harvest Farm in Wellington, Colorado. The event features a corn maze, petting zoo, hayrides, and more! It’s a fun event for the whole family!
