Harvest Farm Fall Festival

The Harvest Farm Fall Festival is an annual event held at Harvest Farm in Wellington, Colorado. The event features a corn maze, petting zoo, hayrides, and more! It’s a fun event for the whole family!

Thanksgiving Banquet-in-a-Box

The Thanksgiving Banquet-in-a-Box event helps families in need have a festive holiday meal at their own home by providing a box of holiday food items and a frozen turkey.

Adopt-A-Family

Adopt-A-Family is a special event hosted by the Mission every Christmas where generous sponsor families purchase, wrap and deliver presents to families in need in the community.
